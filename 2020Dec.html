Happy Holidays!
聖誕快樂!
School is over and the year has almost come to a close. Today is the great conjunction of Jupiter and Saturn. The two vibrant planets will come to a visual closest approach within decades on our evening sky to the south-west horizon. On account of the pandemic, we encourage everyone to stay at home and try to observe this spectacular event while also observing personal safety measures.
一年過去，又嚟到放假嘅時候喇！今晚傍晚喺西南面近地平線嘅天空可以睇到難得一見、木星同土星幾十年內視覺上最近嘅嘅土木合相；不過為大家健康着想，要睇嘅話都係盡量留喺屋企，並且保持社交距離喇~
